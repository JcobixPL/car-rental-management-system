# System Modules

## Authentication

Handles sign-in, password authentication, one-time codes and staff two-factor authentication.

## Users and Roles

Handles administrator, employee and customer user accounts, roles and permissions.

## Customers

Handles customer profiles, contact data, identification data, account status and blacklist status.

## Branches

Handles rental branches and employee assignment to branches.

## Vehicles

Handles vehicle registration, vehicle data, current branch, mileage, status and availability.

## Vehicle Categories

Handles vehicle categories, category pricing, deposits and category hierarchy.

## Vehicle Cases

Handles vehicle issues, maintenance, inspections, administrative matters, work orders, costs and invoices.

## Reservations

Handles customer reservations, reservation approval, vehicle assignment, cancellation and reservation changes.

## Rentals

Handles vehicle pickup, active rentals, rental extensions, vehicle return and rental completion.

## Payments

Handles simulated payments, payment attempts, outstanding balances and refunds.

## Pricing

Handles rental pricing, optional service pricing, insurance pricing and configurable pricing rules.

## Audit Logs

Handles the history of important user actions and changes to sensitive or business-critical data.
