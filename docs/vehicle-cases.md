# Vehicle Cases

## Purpose

Vehicle cases are used to track issues, maintenance, inspections and administrative work related to vehicles.

## Vehicle Availability

When creating or updating a vehicle case, an employee decides whether the case blocks the vehicle from reservations and rentals.

A blocking case makes the vehicle unavailable for the configured date range.

A non-blocking case allows the vehicle to remain available.

## Case Schedule

A vehicle case has a planned start date and planned end date.

The dates can be updated while the case is active.

Changes to the case schedule must be recorded in the audit history.

## Case Management

An employee can manage a vehicle case during its lifecycle.

A case can include:

- description,
- category,
- status,
- planned dates,
- blocking availability flag,
- assigned employee,
- comments,
- completed work orders,
- costs,
- invoice records.

## Work Orders

Employees can add work orders completed during a vehicle case.

A work order can include:

- title,
- description,
- completion date,
- cost,
- employee or external service provider.

## Costs and Invoices

Employees can manually add costs and invoice records related to a vehicle case.

An invoice record can include:

- invoice number,
- issuer,
- issue date,
- amount,
- description,
- optional document attachment.

The MVP does not include full accounting or invoice validation.
